C&H Sugar / ASR

At C&H Sugar / ASR, I am responsible for creating the work instruction docummentation for the packaging lines. These documents are used as training and reference material for new employees while they learn how to operate new stations.

To complete the instructions, I spend anywhere from 7 - 30 days interviewing the subject matter experts, working to understand and lay out the information I receive as clearly as possible.

Additionally, I also photograph, and edit, each step of the process for use in the work instructions.

Samples of my work for C&H / ASR are available upon request. Please contact me directly for examples.
